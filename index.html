The Warm Heart of Africa

16-Day Ultimate Malawi Tour Itinerary

Full Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival
  • Meet and greet at the airport
  • Transfer to the Sunbird Capital Hotel (two nights)
  • Relax and rest after flight
  • 7:00 PM: Welcome Dinner
Day 2: Lilongwe City Tour
  • Visit Memorial Tower & Kamuzu Mausoleum
  • Visit Parliament Building & Bingu National Stadium
  • Visit Local Craft Market & Densely populated vendor market
  • Visit Bunda Hill
Day 3: Chongoni Rock Art & Gule Wamkulu
  • Head to Dedza for Chongoni Rock Art
  • Visit Chipalukwa Village
  • Watch Gule Wamkulu traditional dance
  • Transfer to Mua Mission (Namalikhate Lodge)
Day 4: Kungoni Cultural Museum & Cape Maclear
  • Cultural Museum tour & Craft Gallery
  • Watch traditional cultural dances
  • 11:00 AM: Transfer to Cape Maclear (Gecko Lounge)
Day 5: Lake Malawi Boat Trip
  • Boat trip to Thumbi Island and Otters Point
  • African Fish Eagle watching
  • Feed the colorful cichlids
Day 6: Liwonde National Park
  • Transfer to Liwonde National Park (Kutchire Lodge)
  • 4:00 PM: Sunset Boat Safari
  • 6:30 PM: Dinner at the lodge
Day 7: Game Drive & Malape Pillars
  • 5:30 AM: Morning Big Five Game Drive
  • 2:30 PM: Visit Malape Pillars (45 min drive)
Day 8: Zomba City
  • Transfer to Zomba (Colonial Capital)
  • Visit Chilema Tree & Botanical Gardens
  • Check in at Kefi Café Hotel
Day 9: Williams Falls & Blantyre
  • Visit Williams Falls & Mulunguzi Dam on Zomba Plateau
  • 11:00 AM: Transfer to Blantyre
  • Check in at Malawi Sun Hotel
Day 10: Blantyre City & Satemwa Tea
  • St. Michaels of All Angels Church & Mandala House
  • 1:30 PM: Satemwa Tea Estate Plantation & Factory tour
Day 11: Mulanje Mountain
  • Head to Mulanje District (Nalipiri Eco Lodge)
  • Relax with tea plantation views
  • Township walking tour
Day 12: Dziwe Lankhalamba (Pool of the Elder)
  • Visit the natural rock pool at the foot of the mountain
  • Swimming, photos, and lunch by the water
Day 13: Majete Game Reserve
  • 9:00 AM: Transfer to Majete
  • Check in at Ng’ona Lodge
  • 4:00 PM: Sunset Safari
Day 14: Game Drive & Village Safari
  • Early morning game drive
  • 2:00 PM: Local village walking tour
Day 15: Return to Blantyre
  • Transfer to Blantyre (Malawi Sun Hotel)
  • Final evening for relaxation and souvenirs
Day 16: Departure
  • Breakfast and airport transfer
  • End of Service
