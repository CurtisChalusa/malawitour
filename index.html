16-Day Malawi Tour

Explore the Warm Heart of Africa

The Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival
  • Meet and greet at the airport
  • Transfer to the Sunbird Capital Hotel (two nights)
  • Relax and rest after flight
  • 7:00 PM: Welcome Dinner
Day 2: Lilongwe City Tour
  • Memorial Tower & Kamuzu Mausoleum
  • Parliament Building & Bingu National Stadium
  • Local Craft Market & Densely populated vendor market
  • Bunda Hill visit
Day 3: Chongoni Rock Art & Gule Wamkulu
  • Head to Dedza for Chongoni rock art
  • Visit Chipalukwa village
  • Watch Gule Wamkulu traditional dance
  • Check in at Namalikhate Lodge (Mua Mission)
Day 4: Kungoni Cultural Museum & Cape Maclear
  • Cultural museum tour & local craft gallery
  • Traditional dance performances
  • 11:00 AM: Transfer to Cape Maclear (Gecko Lounge)
Day 5: Lake Malawi Boat Trip
  • Boat trip to Thumbi Island and Otters Point
  • African Fish Eagle watching
  • Feeding cichlids & Snorkeling
Day 6: Liwonde National Park
  • Transfer to Liwonde (Kutchire Lodge)
  • 4:00 PM: Sunset Boat Safari
Day 7: Game Drive & Malape Pillars
  • 5:30 AM: Morning Game Drive (Big Five)
  • 2:30 PM: Visit Malape Pillars
Day 8: Zomba City (The Old Capital)
  • Transfer to Zomba
  • Visit Chilema Tree & Colonial buildings
  • Botanical Gardens
  • Check in: Kefi Café Hotel
Day 9: Williams Falls & Zomba Plateau
  • Visit Williams Falls & Mulunguzi Dam
  • Flora and Fauna exploration
  • 11:00 AM: Transfer to Blantyre (Malawi Sun Hotel)
Day 10: Blantyre City & Satemwa Tea Estate
  • St. Michaels of All Angels Church
  • Mandala House (Oldest building in Malawi)
  • Satemwa Tea Estate tour & Factory visit
Day 11: Mulanje Mountain
  • Head to Mulanje District
  • Check in: Nalipiri Eco Lodge
  • Township walking tour & tea plantation views
Day 12: Dziwe Lankhalamba (Pool of the Elder)
  • Visit the natural pool at the foot of Mulanje
  • Swimming & Picnic lunch
Day 13: Majete Game Reserve
  • 9:00 AM: Transfer to Majete
  • Check in: Ng’ona Lodge
  • 4:00 PM: Sunset Safari
Day 14: Game Drive & Village Walk
  • Morning Game Drive
  • 2:00 PM: Village walking tour
Day 15: Return to Blantyre
  • 9:30 AM: Transfer to Blantyre (Malawi Sun Hotel)
  • Afternoon at leisure
Day 16: Departure
  • Transfer to the airport
  • End of service
